# Wordle

version 1.2, 2022-2-15, by YiMo Xia, <yimoxia@outlook.com>

## How to play ?

***

- 1. Open wordle.py.
- 2. Choose your level.
- 3. You can click red "x" to back to the choosing page.
- 4. Look at the tip of the title.
- 5. Click QUIT or click ESC to exit.
